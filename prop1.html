About Big Town

Big Town Sports City

Big Town Sports City is a luxurious residential complex located in Mumbai by Big Town Group. It’s an Under Construction project, spread over an area of 15 acres.  It has 10 towers with a range of 2BHK (632Sqft, 639Sqft) and 3BHK ( 812Sqft) apartments.

Big Town Sports City is equipped with various resident-centric amenities that include Reserved Parking, Gymnasium, and Power Backup.
